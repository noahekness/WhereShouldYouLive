This is a Streamlit app that allows users to filter data by city and see detailed information about that city.

## Audience
Provide simple comparison of cities for young people starting their career.
People transitioning from campus life to adult-city life may struggle to adapt to unforeseen challenges.
The goal is to find them a city that fits their career, budget, and lifestyle.
My role is an advisor but also a peer who will use this information to make informed decisions myself.


## How to run
- Run `streamlit run WhereShouldYouLive.py` in the terminal and it will open in localhost on your preferred browser

## Note on data:
- Data was manually collected from various sources, some state level and some city level.
- There are 23 main cities that I will be looking at
- I have a lot of different sources of data streams, mostly collected manually. All are listed at the bottom with active links.
